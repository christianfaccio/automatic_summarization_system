The Spanish Ministry of Education and Vocational Training has announced the budget for the academic year 2024-2025, amounting to 1920 million euros. The regulation governing this budget is Real Decreto 201/2024, dated February 27, 2024. This budget covers various educational programs, including university programs leading to official degrees and master's titles, as well as non-university programs such as secondary education, vocational training, artistic and sports education, religious studies, language studies, access and preparation courses, and basic grade education.

Scholarships will be available for students, with tuition fees covered by public funds. Additionally, there will be a fixed income of 1700 euros and a residency grant of 2500 euros per student. Excellent academic performance will also be rewarded with additional grants, with amounts ranging from 50 to 125 euros depending on the student's GPA. A basic grant of 300 euros and a higher basic grant of 350 euros for students in basic cycle education will also be provided.
