The budget for the academic year 2021-2022 is 2038.13 million euros. The regulation stipulates that the budget must be allocated to programs such as university programs leading to official degree titles and master's degrees, non-university programs like primary school completion courses, professional formation programs, artistic and athletic training programs, religious studies, language learning at official state schools, access preparation tests, and formativos grade basic cycle courses. Scholarship components include tuition coverage by public fees, fixed income in euros of 1700, residency income of 1600, excellence by GPA with varying amounts between 50, 75, 100, and 125 points, respectively, depending on the student's GPA range, a basic grant of 300 euros, a basic grant for basic cycles of 350 euros, and a variable minimum amount of 60 euros. The academic requirement for universities is a minimum of 60 credits per program, while the minimum GPA required is 0.
