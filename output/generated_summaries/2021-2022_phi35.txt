The Spanish educational budget for the fiscal year of 2021–2022 was set at €2038.13 million according to Regulation No. 471/2021 from June 29th. The funding is allocated across various programs including university degrees leading to official titles in both undergraduate ('Enseñanzas universitarias') and postgraduate levels ('Máster'), as well as specialized courses like Preparatory University Access Courses aimed specifically at individuals over 25 years old. Non-degree offerings such as Secondary School Level Bachelor's Programmes, Vocational Training Certificates ranging from medium to higher education stages, Artistic Professional Studies, Sports Education, Higher Arts & Religious Superior Studies also receive financial support within this framework. Additionally, language instruction provided by state certified schools falls into these categories too. Students seeking admission through entrance exams will find resources available during their application window which commenced on October 14th but extended until December 31st due to exceptional circumstances allowing late submissions beyond September end date.
