The Real Decreto 163/2025, issued on March 4, 2025, regulates the educational programs and scholarships for the academic year 2025-2026 in Spain. The budget for this academic year is none million euros. The programs covered under this decree include university-level studies such as artistic higher education, undergraduate and master's degrees, preparation courses for university access for older students, and complementary formation for master's degrees and credit acquisition. Non-university programs consist of primary and secondary bachelor's degrees, vocational training cycles at intermediate and superior levels, professional arts education, sports studies, religious studies at the superior level, language studies at official institutions, access and preparation courses for various examinations, and basic grade cycles.

The scholarship components include tuition fees covered by public funds, a fixed income of €1,700, residence expenses of €2,700, variable grants based on GPA ranging from €50 to €125, a basic grant of €300, a basic grant for basic cycle students of €350, and a minimum grant of €60. Academic requirements include completing a minimum of 60 credits and maintaining a minimum GPA of 5.0. The application period runs from March 24, 2025, until December 31
